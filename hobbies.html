Favorite Virus?

CIH, 100%, its one of the most destructive viruses. It caused over $250 million worth of damage, and that isn't all over lost data and software, but actual hardware damage. Due to a bug in some motherboards around the time, you were able to reflash the bios on those boards. So, what the software does is that it destroys the FAT on the disk then wipes the BIOS from the boards that can be affected. Its amazing and Dannoct1 has 2 good videos on the subject.
